New Nintendo 3DS extended control contract

1. Hardware availability must be confirmed before any C-Stick or ZL/ZR action is emitted.
2. Failed or missing detection must map all extended actions to zero.
3. Old Nintendo 3DS controls remain available in every scheme.
4. SELECT+Y changes only the extended control scheme and must not sell a tower.
5. Opposite digital C-Stick directions cancel to a neutral axis.
6. Extended input changes presentation and control mapping only; gameplay simulation remains deterministic.
